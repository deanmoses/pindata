name: Automatic Games
---

Automatic Games Company was a small [[technology-generation:pure-mechanical]] pin-game maker with a brief, geographically split presence in the early 1930s. IPDB records place the company in both St. Paul, Minnesota and Inglewood, California, with just two known models: *[[title:hi-lo-4]]* (1932) and *[[title:fifty-grand]]* (1933).
