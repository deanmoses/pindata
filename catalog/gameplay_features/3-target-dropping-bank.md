A bank of three targets that drop when struck, classified separately from the standard [[gameplayfeature:3-bank-drop-targets]] designation. IPDB catalogs approximately 8 machines with this configuration, including *[[title:black-knight-2000]]* (1989) by [[manufacturer:williams]] and *[[title:pinbot]]* (1986). These dropping banks function similarly to conventional [[gameplayfeature:drop-targets]] but may differ in their mechanical implementation or playfield integration, with the bank resetting after all three targets have been cleared.
