A [[gameplayfeature:bank-drop-targets]] configuration with six [[gameplayfeature:drop-targets]] in a row. Occupying a middle ground between the common five-bank and the larger seven-bank, the six-bank saw moderate use across a range of manufacturers. IPDB catalogs approximately 21 machines with this arrangement, including *[[title:doctor-who]]* (1992) and *[[title:fathom]]* (1981). The six-target row was well-suited to spelling six-letter words or representing dual three-target sequences, giving designers flexibility in how they structured the completion objective.
