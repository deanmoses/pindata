A [[gameplayfeature:ramps]] with a single entrance and multiple exits, using a diverting mechanism to determine which exit the ball takes on any given shot. The diverter — typically a solenoid-controlled gate or flap positioned at a junction point — routes the ball left or right based on the game's current state, mode, or scoring rules. Diverter ramps give designers the ability to send the same shot to different destinations at different times, adding variety and strategic depth to repeated ramp shots. The player may aim for the same ramp entrance throughout a game but receive different rewards and ball paths depending on when the shot is made.
