---
name: Swinging Clown Target
is_type_of:
  - swinging-targets
---

A type of [[gameplay-feature:swinging-targets]] shaped like a clown figure, combining the timing challenge of a moving target with carnival-themed playfield decoration. The clown swings back and forth across the player's line of fire, requiring a well-timed shot to connect. [[manufacturer:genco]]'s *[[title:big-top-rifle-gallery]]* (1954) featured a swinging clown target as part of its carnival shooting-gallery theme, alongside [[gameplay-feature:moving-ducks]] and [[gameplay-feature:candle-targets]] — a full complement of midway-inspired mechanisms that turned the playfield into a miniature fairground attraction.
