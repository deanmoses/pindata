Novelty [[gameplayfeature:targets]] shaped like eyeballs, designed to reinforce a horror or supernatural theme through their visual appearance on the playfield. When struck, the eyeball target registers a hit like a conventional [[gameplayfeature:standup-targets]], but the sculpted form adds a visceral, thematic impact that a flat rectangular target cannot match. [[manufacturer:data-east]]'s *[[title:tales-from-the-crypt]]* (1993) featured eyeball targets, fitting the macabre source material with a playfield element that stares back at the player — a memorable example of how target design can serve narrative atmosphere as much as game mechanics.
