The most common configuration for [[gameplayfeature:drop-targets]]: multiple targets arranged in a row — typically three, four, or five — that the player must knock down as a group. Completing the entire bank awards a bonus, lights a feature, or advances a game objective, after which the targets reset and the challenge begins again. This completion-and-reset cycle gives bank drop targets their distinctive rhythm, turning each bank into a small puzzle embedded in the larger playfield. Banks appear so frequently that the term "drop targets" almost always implies a bank; a single target standing alone is the less common [[gameplayfeature:solitary-drop-targets]] variant.
