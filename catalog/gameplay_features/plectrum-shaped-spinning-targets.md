A variant of [[gameplay-feature:spinning-targets]] shaped like a plectrum — a guitar pick — rather than the standard rectangular metal plate. The asymmetric, rounded-triangle profile changes how the target interacts with the ball: the curved edges produce a different deflection pattern on contact, and the distinctive shape provides strong thematic reinforcement on music-themed machines. [[manufacturer:jersey-jack]]'s *[[title:guns-n-roses-jersey-jack-pinball]]* (2020) introduced plectrum-shaped spinning targets across all three editions, tying the playfield mechanism directly to the rock-and-roll theme through both form and function.
