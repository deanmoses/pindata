---
name: Timed Multiball
is_type_of:
  - multiball
---

A [[gameplay-feature:multiball]] variant in which multiple balls remain in play for a set duration, regardless of how many balls drain during that time. Unlike standard multiball — which ends once only one ball remains on the playfield — timed multiball keeps feeding new balls into play until the clock expires, shifting the challenge from ball preservation to maximizing scoring within the time window.
