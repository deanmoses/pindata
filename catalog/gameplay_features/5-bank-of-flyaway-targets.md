A bank of five [[gameplayfeature:flyaway-targets]] — [[gameplayfeature:hanging-targets]] that swing upward and lock in the raised position once struck. Completing the full five-target bank required the player to hit each flyaway individually, with each target remaining retracted after being struck. IPDB catalogs approximately 4 machines with this configuration, including *[[title:grand-slam]]* (1983) by [[manufacturer:bally]]. The five-bank was the only standard bank size cataloged for flyaway targets, concentrating this uncommon mechanism into a single, satisfying group-completion objective.
