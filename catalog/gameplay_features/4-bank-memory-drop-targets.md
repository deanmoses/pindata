A bank of four [[gameplay-feature:memory-drop-targets]] — [[gameplay-feature:drop-targets]] that retain their dropped state between turns, allowing the player to complete the bank incrementally across multiple balls. IPDB catalogs approximately 5 machines with this configuration, including *[[title:bad-girls]]* (1988) and the *[[title:stranger-things]]* (2019) series by [[manufacturer:stern-pinball]]. The four-target memory bank extended the carry-over mechanic to a slightly longer sequence, rewarding persistent play across the entire game.
