A [[gameplayfeature:bank-drop-targets]] configuration with four [[gameplayfeature:drop-targets]] in a row. The four-target bank offered designers a slightly longer completion sequence than the ubiquitous three-bank, often used for spelling four-letter words or representing card suits. IPDB catalogs approximately 244 machines with this configuration, making it the second most common bank size. Iconic titles include *[[title:kiss]]* (1979), *[[title:star-trek]]* (1979), and *[[title:xenon]]* (1980), all of which used four-bank drops as central playfield objectives.
