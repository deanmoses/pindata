A row of five [[gameplayfeature:standup-targets]] grouped as a bank — a larger standup bank configuration used to create longer completion sequences. IPDB catalogs only a single machine with this specific designation: *[[title:back-to-the-future]]* (1990) by [[manufacturer:data-east]]. The five-target standup bank offered a wider spread of targets across the playfield than the more common [[gameplayfeature:3-bank-standup-targets]], demanding more shots to complete the full sequence.
