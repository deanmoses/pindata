---
name: Ball Locks
aliases:
  - Ball lock
---

Mechanisms that trap and hold one or more balls on the playfield, removing them from play until enough have been collected to trigger [[gameplay-feature:multiball]]. A ball lock can take many physical forms — a [[gameplay-feature:sinkholes]] that swallows the ball, a [[gameplay-feature:kick-out-saucers]] that holds it in place, or a set of posts that rise to capture it in a lane. The player locks balls one at a time over successive shots; once the required number is reached (typically two or three), the machine releases them all simultaneously, flooding the playfield for high-scoring, chaotic play.
