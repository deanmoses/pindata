A [[gameplay-feature:bank-drop-targets]] configuration with eight [[gameplay-feature:drop-targets]] in a row. Uncommon among major American manufacturers, the eight-bank was favored by European and South American builders, particularly [[manufacturer:zaccaria]] and [[manufacturer:recel]]. IPDB catalogs approximately 10 machines with this arrangement, none with large production runs. The eight-target row created a wide, imposing bank across the playfield, demanding the player clear nearly twice as many targets as a standard four- or five-bank to complete the sequence.
