A bank of five targets that drop when struck, classified separately from the standard [[gameplayfeature:5-bank-drop-targets]] designation. IPDB catalogs approximately 4 machines with this configuration, including *[[title:pinbot]]* (1986) and *[[title:jack-bot]]* (1995) — both part of the [[series:pinbot]] series by [[manufacturer:williams]]. These dropping banks function similarly to conventional [[gameplayfeature:drop-targets]] but may differ in their mechanical arrangement or playfield context, with the bank resetting after all five targets have been cleared.
