A [[gameplayfeature:bank-drop-targets]] configuration with just two [[gameplayfeature:drop-targets]] — the smallest standard bank size. Often used as a secondary objective alongside larger banks, the two-bank provided a quick completion target that could gate access to a feature or light a bonus. IPDB catalogs approximately 20 machines with this arrangement, including *[[title:buck-rogers]]* (1980) and *[[title:disco-fever]]* (1978). The two-bank's compact size made it easy to tuck into tight playfield locations where a larger bank would not fit.
