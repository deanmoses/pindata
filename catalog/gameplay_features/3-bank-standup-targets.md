A row of three [[gameplay-feature:standup-targets]] grouped as a bank. Completing all three targets in the bank typically lights a bonus, advances a game mode, or qualifies a playfield feature. IPDB catalogs approximately 12 machines with this specific configuration, including *[[title:high-speed]]* (1986), *[[title:funhouse]]* (1990), and *[[title:star-trek-the-next-generation]]* (1993). Because standup targets do not retract when hit, the three-bank serves as a persistent completion challenge that resets electronically rather than mechanically.
