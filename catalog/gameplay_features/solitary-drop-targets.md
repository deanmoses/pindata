A single [[gameplayfeature:drop-targets]] standing alone on the playfield rather than arranged in a bank with others. Solitary drop targets serve as individual objectives — hitting one retracts it below the surface to reveal a feature behind it, open a lane, or advance a game mode. Their isolated placement makes them high-value precision shots, often positioned in challenging locations where they guard specific rewards. The solitary configuration strips the drop target mechanic down to its simplest form: one target, one shot, one immediate consequence.
