Four [[gameplayfeature:drop-targets]] arranged in a line rather than a traditional side-by-side [[gameplayfeature:bank-drop-targets]] configuration. The in-line arrangement places targets sequentially along a lane or channel, so the ball drops them one after another as it travels through. IPDB catalogs approximately 3 machines with this configuration, including *[[title:paragon]]* (1979) by [[manufacturer:bally]]. The four-stage in-line sequence was the longest in-line drop target arrangement in common use, creating an extended gauntlet for the ball to clear.
