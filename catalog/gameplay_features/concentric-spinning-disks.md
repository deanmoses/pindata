A more elaborate variant of the standard [[gameplay-feature:spinning-discs]], featuring multiple discs nested concentrically — a smaller disc rotating inside a larger one, each spinning independently. The layered rotation compounds the unpredictability: a ball that contacts the outer disc may be flung inward onto the inner disc, which then redirects it again at a completely different angle and speed. The result is a double dose of chaos that makes ball trajectory even harder to anticipate than a single spinning disc, turning one playfield element into a multi-stage randomizer.
