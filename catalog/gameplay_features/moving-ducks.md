A type of [[gameplay-feature:moving-targets]] adapted from the carnival shooting gallery, featuring motor-driven duck figures that travel across the playfield on a track. The player must time their shot to strike a duck as it moves past, combining pinball skill with the familiar fairground challenge of hitting a moving target in a row. [[manufacturer:genco]]'s *[[title:big-top-rifle-gallery]]* (1954) featured moving ducks as part of its carnival-themed playfield, translating the classic midway duck shoot into a pinball mechanism — a novelty that blurs the line between pinball and arcade shooting gallery.
