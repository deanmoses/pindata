A variant of [[gameplayfeature:spinning-discs]] equipped with an embedded magnet and capable of spinning in both directions. The bidirectional rotation, combined with the magnet's pull on the steel ball, creates a wider range of unpredictable outcomes than a standard spinning disc — the ball may be grabbed, accelerated, or flung off at angles that change depending on the disc's current direction and speed. [[manufacturer:stern-pinball]]'s *[[title:teenage-mutant-ninja-turtles]]* (2020) featured this mechanism on its premium and limited-edition models, adding a layer of magnetic chaos to an already dynamic playfield element.
