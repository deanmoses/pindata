A [[gameplayfeature:standup-targets]] variant in which a single target face is divided into two or more sections, each wired to its own switch. Where a conventional standup target registers one hit regardless of where the ball strikes, a split standup target distinguishes between upper, lower, or side impacts — allowing different scores, different rule advances, or different game responses from what appears to be a single target. The design adds a layer of precision to an otherwise simple mechanism, rewarding players who can aim not just at the target but at the right section of it.
